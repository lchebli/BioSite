Jessica resides in the Mojave Desert near Joshua Tree, California. She has lived there for over ten years. Some more information about the Mojave Desert can be found on the California Welcome Center website. In her spare time, she likes to travel. Jessica enjoys the beach and tries to visit anytime she can. One of her dream places to visit is Scottland. Jessica also has a love for music. She likes various genres, and her playlists will take you anywhere from Classical music to Punk and R&B. She tries to go to concerts whenever she can. She is a great mother of two human kids and two fur babies. Her furry family includes Bruce, a cat and Cosmo, a big German Shepherd. They co-exist peacefully with one another. Jessica likes occasionally going to the movie theaters. She prefers movies not to be really long and have a good story line. She loves comedies, action movies, romance, and fantasy. Getting out of her comfort zone is something she does whenever she can. She loves karaoke and will occasionally go and sing at local events around town. Jessica has been vegan for nine years. She is a great cook and will find vegan substitutes for her favorite dishes while making everything from scratch. Some of her favorite dishes are nachos and tacos. She also loves coffee and needs at least one cup a day to survive.
